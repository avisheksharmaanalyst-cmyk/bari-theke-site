Our Story

There are evenings when you suddenly miss home.
Not just the place — but the familiar flavours, the warmth of the kitchen, the comfort of something made with care.

Bari Theke was created for every Bengali living away from home — for those who long for not just food, but a feeling.

We prepare small-batch Bengali sweets and snacks the way they are made at home — with time, patience, and no shortcuts.

Because food from home should feel like home.
